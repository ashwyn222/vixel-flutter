Account & Data Deletion

App: Vixel — Offline Video Editor

Developer: Ashwin Kumar Sharma

Contact: ashwyn222@gmail.com

You can delete your Vixel account and all associated data at any time, either from inside the app or by emailing us.

Option 1: Delete from inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open Vixel on your device.
  2. Tap the Settings icon (top-right of the home screen).
  3. Scroll to the Account section.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Confirm the action when prompted.

The deletion is performed immediately. You will be signed out and your account will no longer exist.

Option 2: Email request (for users who have uninstalled the app)

Send an email to ashwyn222@gmail.com from the Google account you used to sign in to Vixel, with the subject line "Vixel account deletion request".

We will verify the request and delete your account and associated data within 30 days, and reply to confirm.

What is deleted

DataAction
Firebase Authentication user (your Vixel account, including the email and name we received from Google Sign-In) Permanently deleted
Free-tier operation timestamps (users/<uid> in Cloud Firestore) Permanently deleted
Diagnostic / error reports linked to your Firebase UID (error_logs in Cloud Firestore) Permanently deleted
Local app data and cached files on your device Removed when you uninstall the app, or via your device's app-info screen

What is kept (and for how long)

DataRetentionReason
Purchase & subscription records held by Google Play As long as Google Play retains them Required by Google Play for receipts, refunds and tax compliance. Vixel does not store these records itself.
Firebase / Google Cloud system logs (e.g. server access logs) Up to 30 days Routine operational logs maintained by Google. They are not used by Vixel.

For the full picture of what we collect and how it is used, see our Privacy Policy.
